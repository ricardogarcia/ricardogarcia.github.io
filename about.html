Ricardo Garcia

Ricardo specializes in mobile software platforms and web technology. He's launched multiple early-stage startups by developing their technology platforms from the ground up with limited resources and tight timeframes. Ricardo started EverySignal and built a real time social aggregation platform within 6 months. His latest venture aims to disrupt newsfeed technology by leveraging social connections to find the best niche experiences. Ricardo would like to incorporate more social entrepreneurship into his portfolio. Ideas such as using driverless cars to help feed the homeless by picking up leftover family table portions excite him greatly. Ricardo received a B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
